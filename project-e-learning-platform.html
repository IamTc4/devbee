UI/UX Design • 4 months

Project Overview

An interactive learning management system for schools and businesses. Features include comprehensive course creation tools, individual progress tracking, integrated quizzes, and live video conferencing capabilities.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Designing a scalable architecture capable of supporting **10,000+ simultaneous users**.
  • Developing a complex, interactive quiz and automated grading system.
  • Ensuring seamless, low-latency **video integration** for live classrooms (using WebRTC).
  • Integrating **Gamification** elements (badges, leaderboards) to maximize user engagement.

Technologies Used

  • Sketch
  • React.js
  • WebRTC
  • Node.js
  • PostgreSQL

Results & Impact

  • Course Completion: **+60%** (Higher completion rates due to better UX)
  • User Engagement: **+80%** (More active user sessions and time on platform)
  • Support Tickets: **-30%** (Fewer help requests due to intuitive design)
  • Mobile Usage: **+95%** (Achieved high adoption on mobile devices)
